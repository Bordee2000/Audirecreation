Powerful and dynamic

The Audi TT Coupé is available with powerful 2.0 TFSI engine the 45 TFSI with 169 kW (230 hp). The 45 TFSI is also available with quattro all-wheel drive. The basic equipment includes the Audi drive select driving dynamics system with various driving modes. Depending on the setting, you will feel changes in, for example, the steering, gearshift and engine characteristics.

Sporty and expressive

You will recognise at first glance: The Audi TT Coupé is a real athlete. The single-frame grille with three-dimensional radiator protection grille and large side air inlets at the front and twin tailpipes at the rear are typical features.

Everything at a glance

The cockpit in the Audi TT Coupé is your control centre: receive important information about intelligent technologies. The top version of the operating terminal, the MMI Navigation plus with MMI touch, is controlled by the rotary push-button, whose touchpad on the top recognises your handwriting. You have an overview of all displays in the innovative and standard Audi virtual cockpit. With the standard multi-function steering wheel plus you can easily make adjustments.

12.3 inch color display

Well informed

The Audi virtual cockpit will give you great pleasure. You can read the most important data on a 12.3-inch LCD screen – clearly arranged and displayed in high-resolution. The driver can choose between two modes: in the classic view, the focus is on the speedometer and rev counter, while in the “infotainment” mode, content such as the navigation map is displayed in a larger size.

More about the operating concept & infotainment

Your safety in focus

Thanks to the parking aid plus and rear view camera, you can easily park in any gap.

Pack – and get going

The elongated tailgate of the Audi TT Coupé conceals a luggage compartment volume of 305 litres. So you can safely go on a short holiday for two for several days.
