High-level performance

The heart of the Audi TT RS Coupé is up front: the 2.5-litre TFSI-series 5-cylinder engine with 294 kW (400 hp) of power and 480 Nm of torque, you will pass the acceleration test of 0 to 100 km/h in 3.7 seconds with the top speed is 250 km/h. The standard 7-speed S tronic and the quattro permanent all-wheel drive with TT RS-specific tuning are part and parcel of these top performance specs.

Guaranteed goosebumps

The Audi TT RS Coupé achieves its emphatic performance thanks primarily to our 2.5-litre TFSI engine with 294 kW (400 hp). Through the 1-2-4-5-3 ignition sequence, which involves directly neighbouring cylinders and far apart ones igniting in alternation, our multi-prize winning engine emits an unmistakeable sound.

RS-specific elements

In the Audi TT RS Coupé, the sport suspension enhances the sporty driving characteristics. The Audi magnetic ride system continuously adjusts how the shock absorbers work to suit the road surface and your personal driving style. The fixed RS rear wing increases the contact pressure for better grip. With the standard RS high-performance brake system, deceleration is extremely precise.

Compelling confidence

At the front, the most striking feature of the TT RS Coupé² is the Audi Singleframe with a radiator protective grille in a gloss black honeycomb structure and a matt aluminium-look frame. A large quattro logo is emblazoned in the lower section. An impressive feature on the side is the widened sill with a gloss black inlay. Enjoy the full-throated sound of the RS exhaust system with oval tailpipe trims. The visual features at the rear are rounded off by the gloss black diffuser insert.

Dramatic lighting

At the rear of the Audi TT RS Coupé, Audi Matrix OLED rear lights are a captivating sight. When switched on, the four wafer-thin lighting elements complete a full animation. At the front, we offer headlights with LED or Matrix LED technology on request. You no longer need to constantly turn your high beams on and off when driving at night. Instead, a control unit turns individual diodes on and off in response to different driving situations.

RS in focus

The basic version of the Audi TT RS Coupé² includes numerous RS-specific interior features such as the RS sport leather steering wheel with multifunction plus, which is flattened at the bottom. You select the gearshift setting using the RS selector lever in the centre console. The door sill trims with aluminium inlay and TT RS logo, the stainless steel pedals and the RS embossing on the front seat backrests are also included as standard.

Intuitive to operate

When it comes to infotainment, MMI Navigation plus with MMI touch, which you control using the high-precision locking rotary/push button beneath the gear lever. The interface recognises your handwriting: You can input characters and scroll and zoom with your fingers. Alternatively, you can control the MMI using the voice control system.

Everything at a glance

The standard, fully digital Audi virtual cockpit instrument cluster in the Audi TT RS Coupé² displays RS-specific content among other features. On the G meter, you can see the forces that arise while cornering, braking or accelerating. The shift light indicates that the speed limit in the respective gear has been reached.

High-quality sound

Bang & Olufsen Sound System in the Audi TT RS Coupé In the interior of the Audi TT RS Coupé², the Bang & Olufsen Premium Sound System creates an exceptional sound experience thanks to an amplifier with a total output of 680 watts and twelve high-performance loudspeakers. A microphone detects driving and background noises and adjusts the sound accordingly. Visually, the exclusive loudspeaker covers with aluminium trim and the LED accent lighting on the front subwoofers perfectly complement the high-quality interior of your Audi TT RS Coupé².
