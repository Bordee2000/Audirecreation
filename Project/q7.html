Ready for the new digital age

Thanks to innovative and intuitive infotainment technologies, you can continue your digitised lifestyle in the Audi Q7. Directly in front of you is the MMI Radio plus with MMI touch response supported with Audi smartphone interface for Apple Car Play and Android Auto.

Dynamically on the go

The Audi Q7 effortlessly masters a whole host of challenges, regardless of surface, load and speed, while providing a dynamic driving experience. The series-standard quattro all-wheel drive ensures sporty handling. You can actively influence the vehicle's characteristics via seven different modes of the series-standard Audi drive select driving dynamics system, including the air suspension on the S line model which regulates the height of the vehicle. Equipped with series-standard mild hybrid technology, which ensures efficient driving and thus reduces fuel consumption.

Comfortable and flexible – the interior

Feel great in the Audi Q7 with features such as the multicoloured contour and ambient lighting package for a more impressive atmosphere.

S line

Functional and suitable for everyday use

The Audi Q7 is characterised by different space options. The optional third row seats up to seven passengers. For the maximum storage space of up to 2,050 litres, you can fold down the second and third rows of seats. Take advantage of convenient ways to load and unload the vehicle, such as the sensor-controlled luggage compartment unlocking system, which allows you to operate the boot lid with both hands full by means of a foot gesture.

Powerful and sporty – the exterior

The Audi Q7 impresses with its overall dynamic appearance. For an individualised, even sportier look, opt for the S line exterior package. This includes, for example, front and rear S line bumpers, a sporty accentuated rear underride protection and 21-inch wheels.
