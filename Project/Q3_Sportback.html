Progressive design

Self-confident, extroverted, and sporty – this is the Audi Q3 Sportback. The front is dominated by the large single-frame grill with octagon design and the lateral air intakes. The coupé-like silhouette is clearly visible in the side view. The long, elegant shoulder line forms the dynamic basic shape of the vehicle and emphasises the sportiness. Characteristic of the rear are the flowing sloping roof line, the low rear window, and the sharply cut rear lights. Thanks to our flexible equipment line concept, you can individualise the exterior according to your wishes. The S line package is standard equipment, your Audi Q3 Sportback will be delivered with bumpers in contrasting paintwork, 19-inch wheels in 5 twin-spoke dynamic design, and illuminated door sills with front aluminium inserts and S lettering.

Variable and functional

The Audi Q3 Sportback has a highly variable interior. The rear bench offers space for three people and can be moved longitudinally by 130 millimetres as standard; its triple-divided backrests can be tilted in seven positions. The volume of the luggage compartment ranges from 530 to 1,400 litres. Thanks to electric tailgate, which you can open comfortably with your foot if you have no free hand.
