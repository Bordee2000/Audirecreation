More about innovative technologies

Electrifying


In the Audi e-tron Sportback's all-electric drive system, the front and rear axles each have an electric motor. Thanks to the total output of up to 300 kW (408 hp) and the maximum torque of up to 664 Newton-metres, the vehicle accelerates from 0 to 100 km / h in 5.7 seconds (Combined power consumption in kWh / 100 km¹: 22.7 (NEDC); Combined CO₂ emissions in g / km¹: 0). The top speed is electronically limited to 200 km/h.

Progressive steering assistance

The progressive steering of the Audi e-tron Sportback optimises steering behaviour. In the city you have reduced steering effort when parking or manoeuvering, and on winding roads the agile, precise handling ensures increased control.

Adaptable and comfortable

Technology that adapts to your driving style: the sports adaptive air suspension on the Audi e-tron Sportback automatically adjusts the vehicle's level and damping, enhancing both driving comfort and driving dynamics. The Audi drive select vehicle dynamics system also allows you to alter the driving properties yourself using different modes.

More about driving dynamics

Space for ideas

The Audi e-tron Sportback is a combination of sportiness, functionality and suitability for everyday use. The interior offers plenty of space and possibilities, with a boot volume of 615 litres. When the seats are folded, capacity is increased to as much as 1,655 litres. People in the back seats also have plenty of headroom and legroom.
