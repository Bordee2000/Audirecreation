Beautiful view – whatever your perspective

The sportiness of the Audi A5 Coupé impresses – even before your first drive. Thanks to the new design language of the elegant 2-door model with its wide air inlets and chrome trim, which lend the front end a sporty sharpness. At the side, the sportiness is underscored by sills accentuated in the center; the 19-inch cast aluminum wheels are emphasized by pronounced wheel arches. Horizontal trim in gloss black on the diffuser and trapezoidal tailpipe trims impressively highlight the wide rear.
