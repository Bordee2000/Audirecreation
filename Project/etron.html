Electricity consumption combined in kWh/100 km*: 24.6-23.7 (NEDC); 26.2-22.6 (WLTP) CO₂ emissions combined in g/km:

It's your chance: dive into the exciting world of electromobility with the Audi e-tron and help shape the future. Our first all-electric SUV is your sporty, everyday and safe companion for a new era.

The asynchronous motors can deliver this peak performance of 300 kW (408 hp) and 664 Nm of torque for up to 10 seconds. The front electric motor, which is positioned parallel to the axle for the purpose of optimum packaging, achieves a peak output of 125 kW at 247 Nm of torque. The rear, coaxially positioned motor reaches an output of 140 kW with a torque of 314 Nm. Two-stage planetary gearboxes with one gear range transfer the forces to the axles via the differentials.

Powerful and controlled

You need to experience for yourself the starting performance of the Audi e-tron (combined power consumption in kWh/100 km *: 26.2-22.6 (WLTP); 24.6-23.7 (NEDC); CO2 emissions combined in g/km: 0). Whether you accelerate from a standstill or overtake on the motorway - the power of 265 kilowatts unfolds immediately. Even in reverse, you will feel the high level of torque. Optimum traction ensures a new generation of the quattro drive, which can be switched on when needed. The result: you have full control over the vehicle.

No limits

The new electric quattro drive permanently and fully variably regulates the ideal distribution of drive torques between the two axles. And within a split second. You'll particularly feel the benefits during off-road trips in the country. But even when driving on the road, you can rely on the services of the quattro drive. Even in slippery complications such as when under-steering or oversteering occur and a reaction would be required of you, the technology jumps for you.

Experience sportiness

Whether accelerating on the highway, braking at a traffic light or cornering on a country lane - you can feel the sporty character of the Audi e-tron in almost any driving situation. A key fac-tor for the excellent dynamics is the deep and central installation position of the drive compo-nents. The battery system is optimally adapted to the dimensions of the Audi e-tron and is in the form of a flat, wide block under the passenger area between the axles. Even when driving off-road, the Audi e-tron proves its agility and maneuverability.

The high degree of recuperation, i.e. the recovery of braking energy, plays a considerable part in this. During much more than 90 percent of all decelerations, the Audi e-tron recharges itself via its e-machines. Traction test

We tested the traction of the Audi e-tron on unusual surfaces: in the savanna of Namibia and on snow-covered roads in Lapland. Thanks to the new electric four-wheel drive in combina-tion with modern suspension solutions, the SUV passed with flying colours.

Temperature test

The intelligent thermal management keeps the battery of the electric SUV in its optimal tem-perature window of 25 to 30 degrees Celsius in all situations. Their performance is most effi-ciently developed by the battery at an outside temperature of 20 degrees Celsius. At very low or high outside temperatures, the range of the Audi e-tron is affected because cooling or heating the battery requires additional energy. You will also benefit noticeably from the ther-mal management, as it is part of the air conditioning of the interior.

Use time effectively

The Audi e-tron Charging Service gives you access to the majority of all public charging stations. Not only in Germany, but also in many European countries. After activation in the my-Audi portal, you are authorised to charge.
